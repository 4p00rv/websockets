FAQ

What is the easiest way to get started with the Binary.com API?

View the Code Samples.

What is the easiest way to build my own Binary.com front-end application?

Open a GitHub account, download the GitHub Desktop application, then fork one of Binary.com's front-end applications, such as WebTrader (View app) or any of the other open-source front-ends listed here. Make your code changes in your fork, then publish your fork using the GitHub Pages facility. Note: for SSL and website acceleration for your app, open a free account on Cloudflare.

Will I earn commissions if users sign up to Binary.com using my app?

Yes - please sign up as an affiliate, and insert your affiliate token into the new_account_virtual call in your app.

How else can I earn?

You can sign up as a Payment Agent to process local payments for Binary.com clients in your country. You may automate your payment agent facility using the paymentagent_transfer API call.

Any other way to earn?

You may sell your app on the Binary Shop.

Any further ways to earn...?

If you are prepared for your app to offer higher contract prices than the main Binary.com website, you may add a markup percentage when you Register your App. This markup percentage is a percentage of contract payouts, and is added to all contract prices in your app. At every month-end, the aggregate markup is paid to you. Please sign up as an affiliate and contact your Affiliate Manager to learn more about this facility.
